What is Yodlee Used For? renee.hobbs Tue, 08/26/2025 - 01:56 As a global leader in financial data aggregation and open finance, Yodlee powers the banking, wealth management, and payment tools that help consumers live better financial lives. Leveraging data from over 19,000 data sources, Yodlee enables banks, lenders, and financial technology companies to create personalized digital experiences, release innovative solutions, and make strategic business decisions faster. Yodlee’s platform supports financial institutions and consumers in numerous ways, including: Viewing bank, credit card, and investment accounts in one place Monitoring savings, spending, and debt Receiving on demand account alerts Analyzing cash flow and net worth Improving personal financial management Enabling secure account verification Using alternative credit data for lending decisions Learn more about our commitment to empowering both our clients and their customers. Yodlee Capabilities Yodlee offers financial institutions comprehensive data-driven solutions, including data aggregation, account verification, on demand insights on cash flow, personal financial management (PFM) tools, payment enablement, and alternative credit data services. Open Finance Connectivity Yodlee serves as an open finance provider, utilizing consumer-permissioned data to power meaningful and secure financial experiences. Our Open Finance connections provide financial institutions with the tools to build trust while simultaneously driving innovation. Data Aggregation With financial data aggregation , Yodlee consolidates information from multiple accounts, creating a unified financial picture for customers. From enabling holistic views of consumer finances to generating insights on spending behaviors, our aggregation solutions power banking, wealth management, and analytics use cases. By delivering high-quality, on demand data, Yodlee equips institutions to provide tailored advice and next-best actions. Account Verification In a world of digital finances, securely verifying accounts, ownership, and funds is critical. Yodlee’s Account Verification solution reduces fraud by validating information in real time without requiring micro-deposits. Our technology complies with NACHA rules for secure online payments, helping institutions avoid NSF fees and overdraft risks. Alternative Credit Data Yodlee’s cash flow underwriting and alternative credit scoring solutions allow institutions to offer credit products to underbanked consumers. Using cash flow and transaction data, Yodlee provides a comprehensive view of consumer financial behavior, helping lenders make more accurate assessments. Payment Enablement Yodlee equips financial institutions with powerful payment enablement tools, offering functionalities like secure account verification, instant payment authorization, and fraud prevention. These capabilities validate seamless, secure transactions for consumers and businesses. Transaction Data Enrichment Yodlee’s Transaction Data Enrichment turns raw transaction data into clear, actionable insights. By categorizing and contextualizing ambiguous transactions, financial institutions can better understand customer behavior, while consumers gain clarity around their spending patterns. Personal Financial Management Helping consumers achieve financial wellness remains a critical goal for Yodlee. Our PFM solutions analyze broad datasets to deliver personalized financial insights, actionable recommendations, and tools that empower customers to improve their overall financial health. Wealth Management Solutions Yodlee supports wealth management services with solutions like asset transfer verification and wealth aggregation . These tools enable firms to deliver optimal experiences for high-value clients, aligning with their strategic goals while ensuring seamless operations. Who Uses Yodlee? Yodlee provides comprehensive data solutions across multiple industries, including retail banking, merchants and payment providers, wealth management firms, technology companies, and lenders. More than 1,500 financial institutions and technology companies, including 14 of the top 20 U.S. banks, trust Yodlee to power their financial applications. Major companies leveraging Yodlee’s data aggregation and financial solutions include: American Express – Leading global payments network Chase – One of the largest national banks PayPal – Global digital payments platform Empower: Digital wealth management and retirement provider Yahoo Finance’s My Money – Leading online financial news and tools provider For over 25 years, Yodlee has empowered institutions to offer transformative services, helping them grow their business and improve financial wellness for customers.
